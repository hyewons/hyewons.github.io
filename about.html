Hyewon Son

Hyewon is a User Experience Designer based in Seattle.

Prior to entering design, Hyewon worked as a Digital Marketing Specialist at a video analytics software company where she focused on closing the gap between the product and the users. This entailed conducting research on target customer groups, creating effective content strategy for multi-channel platforms, and keeping track of the key metrics to determine success.

Now as a designer, Hyewon aims to directly impact the product growth and design user-friendly solutions to enhance people’s daily experiences. She values empathy in creating designs that speak to the audience. On top of her human-centered design process and prototyping skills, Hyewon is able to define and understand metrics to drive impact and think in terms of business growth.

From September, Hyewon is pursuing a Master’s degree in Human-Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) at the University of Washington. Her prime focus is research-driven design and her core skills include prototyping, information architecture and research. Outside of class, she strives to embrace and learn diverse technologies such as Framer and practice interaction design that meets current design trends.
