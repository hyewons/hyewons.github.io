UW Food Waste Website

Project duration: 6 months. Currently in progress. Watch out for updates!

Food waste is a growing problem globally, with an increasing number of people struggling with food insecurity. Backed by the UW’s award winning Campus Sustainability Fund, the project proposes to build and design a website platform to pair-up UW food waste with local non-profit agencies in need. The open-source website would facilitate the logistics and transportation of food waste from UW Food Services without having so many food products go to compost.
